01 — about

Started at 4
on my brother's
computer.

It started at age 4 — sitting on my brother's lap working on his computer, messing with melodies. At 6 I wrote my first "track": just a collection of cool sounds, but my brother saw something interesting in it.

Then came my own computer, hours of dragging notes around in an editor. The old PC broke. I left music behind — until I downloaded FL Studio again and started messing around.

First nickname: Jonathan Fortile. Then Bearonic, BEARN... until I landed on nxttu. (next to you / nty). Came up with it in class thinking about a friend sitting next to me.

Now — experimental electronic producer from Ukraine. Open for collabs & deals.
